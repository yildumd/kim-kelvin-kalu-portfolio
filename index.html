About Me

A distinguished Public Relations Executive with over two decades of experience working across Nigeria's leading public institutions and international organizations.

Kim Kelvin Kalu is a distinguished Public Relations Executive, Government Communicator, and Media Strategy Professional with over two decades of experience working across Nigeria's leading public institutions and international organizations in the United Kingdom.

She is recognized for her exceptional command of communication strategy, public messaging, stakeholder engagement, and reputation management—skills that make her a trusted asset for leadership roles at the highest levels of government.

Kim is respected for her clarity, emotional intelligence, discretion, and strategic thinking. She excels at shaping narratives, managing high-pressure communications, and translating leadership vision into messages the public can understand, trust, and support.
